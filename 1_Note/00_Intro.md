## Course Content

The OSI (Open Systems Interconnection) Model  
layers:  

1. application(应用层)
2. presentation(表示层)  
3. session(会话层)
4. transport(传输层)
5. network(网络层)
6. data link(数据链路层)
7. physical(物理层)

end system/ host:  
owns the complete 7 layers  

router:  
only the last 3 layers
